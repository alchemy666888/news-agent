# Evaluating the Market Potential of a News & Information Flow AI Agent

**Prepared by:** Marketing Research Lead (10+ years experience)  
**Objective:** Assess commercial viability and determine optimal domain focus

---

# 1. Executive Summary

This marketing research plan is designed to:

1. Evaluate the **market demand and commercial value** of a News & Information Flow AI Agent.
    
2. Identify **high-value domains or verticals** where the agent can achieve strong product-market fit.
    
3. Validate **willingness to pay**, user pain points, and competitive positioning.
    
4. Define a **go-to-market direction** based on evidence.
    

The output of this research will guide:

- Domain focus (e.g., finance, crypto, legal, AI, biotech, geopolitics)
    
- Target customer segment
    
- Pricing strategy
    
- Differentiation positioning
    

---

# 4. Hypotheses

## H1: High-value professionals are willing to pay for structured information.

## H2: Domains with financial impact (e.g., trading, investing, regulation) have higher willingness to pay.

## H3: Crypto and AI domains show higher early-adopter interest.

## H4: Enterprise clients value workflow integration over raw summaries.
